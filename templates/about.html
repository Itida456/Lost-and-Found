About Campus Lost & Found

Our Mission

Campus Lost & Found is dedicated to reconnecting students with their lost belongings efficiently and securely. We understand how disruptive losing personal items can be to student life, and we're here to help.

How It Works

Our platform uses smart matching technology to connect lost item reports with claims. When you lose an item, report it through our system. If someone finds and reports it, you'll be notified to collect it from the campus lost and found office.

Our Team

Campus Lost & Found is run by the Student Services Department, with dedicated staff monitoring reports and managing the physical lost and found office. The technical platform is maintained by our IT department to ensure a seamless experience.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All personal information is securely stored and only used for the purpose of returning lost items to their rightful owners. We never share your information with third parties.
